Scrambled Eggs
Eggs are one of my favorite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

Method
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2 tbs cream
  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over medium heat.
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl.
  3. Once the butter has melted, add the cream and eggs.
  4. Use a spatula to fold the eggs from the edge to the center every 20 seconds.
  5. Remove from heat while still moist (it will keep cooking on the plate).
